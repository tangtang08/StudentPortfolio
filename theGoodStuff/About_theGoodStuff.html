About The Programmer

This summer I'm enrolled in TK2 coding bootcamp. I will graduate as a Full Stack JAVA Developer. I'm also finishing my Computer Science degree in Programming & analysis. Before pursing my degree in Programming, I worked in Post Production editing for small productions, and as a Producer for Independent productions. I also worked as an Assistant Editor & Post Coordinator for companies like ABC, NBC & MTV in L.A as a freelance editor. I then formed a small company doing lead generation working with companies in the states and overseas.


I spent my life being fascinated with the way computers function. After being the 'go to computer person ' for friends and family, I finally decided to my passion to the next level, and starting to learn programming. I went to Broward College to get my degree in Programming and Analysis. While I was attending I decided to accelerate my learning curve. I attended TK2 coding Bootcamp, to become a Full Stack JAVA Developer.

Because I always enjoying learning and finding solutions to challenges and problems, I fell in love with programming. During this journey, I won 2 awards at 2 different Hackthons and had articles writing about my achievements. I look forward to furthering my career as a Jr. Full Stack JAVA Programmer, with a forward-thinking company. I would like to share my skills as a Jr. Programmer, who is open-minded and ready to grow even more.
